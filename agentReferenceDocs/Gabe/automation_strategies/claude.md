### **The Ejection Cable Network**
Part ejection creates the fundamental automation dependency chain:

```
Part_Geometry ‚Üí Bed_Contact_Area ‚Üí Ejection_Force_Required ‚Üí Mechanism_Complexity ‚Üí 
Automation_Feasibility ‚Üí Production_Scalability ‚Üí Economic_Viability
```

**Cable Trace: Large Bed Contact Area**
- Large flat base ‚Üí high bed adhesion
- High adhesion ‚Üí high ejection force required
- High force ‚Üí complex ejection mechanism needed
- Complex mechanism ‚Üí automation difficulty increases
- Automation difficulty ‚Üí manual intervention required
- Manual intervention ‚Üí scalability failure
- **Solution**: Minimize bed contact through geometric redesign

### **The Lights-Out Production Cable Network**
